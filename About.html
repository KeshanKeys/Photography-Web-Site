About Malcolm Lismore

Malcolm Lismore is a passionate freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland. With a deep connection to the natural world, he captures the rugged beauty of the Scottish landscape, the charm of its wildlife, and the grace of coastal birds.

But Malcolm's talent extends beyond landscapes and wildlife. He excels in photographing weddings, portraits, and special events, infusing his work with creativity, emotion, and authenticity.

With years of experience and a keen eye for detail, Malcolm's photography brings the natural world and human moments to life, allowing viewers to connect with Scotland's beauty and their own cherished memories.
